PillPivot

PillPivot is an ergonomic pill bottle designed for people with rheumatoid arthritis in the hands. The medical device aids them in taking one medication needed to manage arthritis. I created the low-fidelity prototype using primarily cardboard. This was used in user testing where the speed and accuracy of the pill box was tested and compared to a pre-determined critical value. This value was based on prior research done. The prototype was not perfect and parts needed to be re-glued or recut for better user experience and testing results.
